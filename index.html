Who We Are

Our Vision

Afrinova X Academy is a trailblazing initiative committed to bringing cutting-edge tech education to classrooms across Africa. We envision a continent where every child, regardless of background, has the skills and confidence to thrive in an AI-driven world.

Our Mission

Empower. Educate. Elevate.

  • Empower: Fuel curiosity and creativity through immersive, student-centered learning.
  • Educate: Deliver structured AI literacy workshops, hands-on projects, and speaker sessions led by industry experts.
  • Elevate: Build lasting partnerships with edtech leaders, schools, and tech communities to scale our impact continent-wide.

Why It Matters

Today's learners are tomorrow's leaders. As schools increasingly embrace technology, we see budding innovators eager to push boundaries. Afrinova X Academy provides the platform for them to collaborate with peers and professionals, build confidence through guided learning, and create solutions that address local and global needs.
