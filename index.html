Who We Are

Afrinova X Academy is a dynamic AI startup on a mission to transform learning and innovation across Africa. Whether you’re a school, a growing enterprise, or an ambitious professional, we deliver tailored AI literacy, hands-on experiences, and strategic consulting to propel you forward.

Our approach combines cutting-edge technology with deep understanding of local contexts to create solutions that are both innovative and practical.
